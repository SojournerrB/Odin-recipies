Cranberry Sauce

------------------

Cranberry sauce is a classic Thanksgiving side-dish. Made from just three simple ingredients, cranberry sauce goes great with turkey, stuffing, or even on it's own!

Let's take a look at how to make this deliciously simple dish.

Cranberry Sauce Ingredients

  • Fresh Cranberries
  • Sugar
  • Orange Juice

Instructions

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Dissolve the sugar in the orange juice in a sauce pan over medium heat.
  3. Stir cranberries into sugar-orange juice mixture and cook until it begins to pop and thicken. This may take about 8-10 minutes.
  4. Remove from the heat and place into a sauce bowl. It will thicken as it cools.
  5. Cranberry sauce is finished!

Note: Garnish the sauce with strips of orange peel or orange zest!
